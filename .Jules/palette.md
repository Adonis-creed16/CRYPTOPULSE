# Palette's Journal - UX & Accessibility Learnings

## 2025-05-08 - Explicit Loading States and Stale Data Awareness
**Learning:** Users often feel anxious when they don't know if the data they are looking at is fresh, especially in real-time applications like crypto trackers. Providing a "Last Updated" timestamp and a clear loading feedback on manual refresh actions reduces this friction and improves trust.
**Action:** Always include a timestamp for data freshness and ensure buttons have "loading" states (disabled + text change) to prevent double-submissions and provide immediate feedback.
